Caution: Fraudulent Websites and Scams



April 5, 2021 - Since the Bitcoin price broke new all-time highs this year, there has been an increase in the number of fraud attempts impersonating BitRiver.

​Although we have not received reports of such attempts being successful so far, we would like to proactively advise all to practice more caution when seeking information about BitRiver or trying to communicate with us. More notably, please remember:

  • Our only official website is https://bitriver.farm/
    When you want to visit our website, please type bitriver.farm or bookmark the website https://bitriver.farm/ to ensure that you are not misled by a website that looks similar to our website or has a URL that very closely resembles our real URL.
  • Our only official social media accounts are on Twitter, LinkedIn and Facebook. Links to these accounts, respectively, are below:
    https://twitter.com/BitRiverMining
    https://www.linkedin.com/company/bitrivermining/
    https://www.facebook.com/BitRiverMining
  • If there is a website or platform that is officially authorised by us to represent BitRiver or sell BitRiver’s products or solutions, we will announce it through either our website or our Twitter account.
To stay informed on latest announcements and updates from BitRiver, please follow https://bitriver.farm/en/newsroom
