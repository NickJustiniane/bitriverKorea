Why customers choose us

The largest mining data center in the CIS

We know how to operate data centers on a large scale – our founders have done it for more than a decade. Currently, we are hosting over 25,000 devices in our data center, with available slots to plug in a lot more devices right away!

Reliability. and Stability

We aim for a long-term and reliable operation and the size of our data center makes it easier for us to do that. We host and service our customers’ equipment on a contract basis. We buy electricity at wholesale price. We actively cooperate with the Bratsk city administration and contribute to the city’s development. We also pay all our due taxes.

99.9% uptime. Guranteed!

We contractually guarantee 99.9% of network access time, and uninterrupted power supply at least 99% of the time. We use two independent fiber optic uplinks alongside a third wireless back up for network redundancy.

In-house repair center

We understand the impact that inoperative devices can have on your business. Therefore, we have gone a step farther by having an in-house repair center with stocked spare parts to fix whatever we can immediately on-site without the need to physically send the equipment to the manufacturer’s repair facility.

Individual power meters

Individual power meters are installed on each server rack. They are installed directly on the switchboards feeding your server racks, so you are not billed for the energy consumed by ventilation or other points of energy consumption in a large-scale data center like ours.

On-site and remote monitoring

Our facility has an integrated 24/7 equipment monitoring and repair system. Around-the-clock guards ensure additional security of your devices. We also provide you with secure online access to your devices, so you are always in full control, including all real-time settings of every device.

Exclusive procurement services

We understand that it is not always easy to procure the best mining hardware, especially in time to meet your business plans. Therefore, we even provide procurement services to those clients who require it. Thanks to years of purchasing various miners in bulk directly from the manufacturers, we have dedicated account managers at the manufacturers and are well versed in the international procurement process.
