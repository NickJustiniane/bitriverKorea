ViaBTC Pool Announces Strategic Partnership with BitRiver

ViaBTC Pool to setup a new physical server at BitRiver’s datacenter in Bratsk, and also offer special terms for increased mining profits to customers of BitRiver’s current and future datacenter facilities

HONG KONG, November 11, 2020 - Cryptocurrency mining pool ViaBTC announces an international strategic partnership with BitRiver, the largest colocation services provider for cryptocurrency mining in Russia and the CIS countries, to provide special terms to those who are customers of BitRiver’s data center facilities. According to the partnership, ViaBTC will also install a new server of its pool in BitRiver’s datacenter in Bratsk before the start of 2021.

Cryptocurrency miners whose mining devices are hosted at BitRiver’s datacenter facilities can now receive special terms from ViaBTC that would make cryptocurrency mining using ViatBTC’s mining pool more profitable to them. Furthermore, installation of a physical server by ViaBTC at BitRiver’s datacenter in Bratsk will ensure that BitRiver’s customers who choose to mine using ViaBTC’s pool have a more reliable and higher quality connection between their mining devices at BitRiver’s facilities and the pool.

“Russia and CIS countries are an important part of our global expansion, and BitRiver is our preferred datacenter for those who host their devices in that region,” said Mr. Haipo Yang, Founder and CEO of ViaBTC. “With this partnership, we look forward to growing our strategic cooperation with BitRiver to better serve our customers and reach more customers globally.”

“It has been our pleasure to work with ViaBTC Pool’s team in the past. This partnership will give global customers of our datacenter facilities more and better options, providing them an even better mining experience,” said Mr. Igor Runets, Founder and CEO of BitRiver.

About ViaBTC: ViaBTC mining pool is the fifth largest BTC mining pool and is also the largest BCH mining pool in the world. ViaBTC has served more than one million users from 130+ countries/regions with secure & professional services of cryptocurrency mining. It pioneered the PPS+ revenue distribution model, which is safe and stable with higher revenue. ViaBTC is also the world’s first mining pool that supports the Hourly Auto Conversion between mined coins and USDT. For more information, visit www.viabtc.com

About BitRiver: Founded by Igor Runets in 2017 and headquartered in Moscow, BitRiver LLC provides hosting services and turnkey solutions for large-scale cryptocurrency mining operations to institutional investors around the world. BitRiver employs over 50 full-time staff in three offices across Russia and full-time sales representatives in China, Japan, UAE, and the USA. With a vision of powering the most promising tech of today and tomorrow through low cost and sustainable energy, BitRiver currently utilizes only surplus hydroelectric power to operate the largest data center that offers co-location services for cryptocurrency mining in Russia and the CIS region.
